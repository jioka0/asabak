The Human Element

Building High-Performance Teams

Innovation is fundamentally a team sport. The most successful innovations emerge from diverse groups of people who bring different perspectives, skills, and experiences to the table. But diversity alone is not enough—teams need the right structure, culture, and processes to harness that diversity effectively.

Our analysis of high-performing innovation teams revealed several key characteristics:

  • Psychological Safety: Team members feel comfortable taking risks and sharing unconventional ideas
  • Clear Purpose: Everyone understands how their work contributes to the larger mission
  • Rapid Iteration: Quick feedback loops that enable fast learning and adaptation
  • Resource Flexibility: Ability to reallocate resources based on emerging opportunities
